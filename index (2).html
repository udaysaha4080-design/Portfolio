§ 01 — Biography

A mind shaped
by questions,
not answers.

Uday is a researcher at the intersection of genetics, evolutionary biology, and theoretical thought. He is not merely learning biology — he is constructing a personal cosmology of life.

"Science, for me, is a form of slow attention — the discipline of noticing what life is actually doing."

His academic roots lie in Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology at Daffodil International University, Dhaka. But his intellectual territory extends far beyond institutional boundaries.
